Where to Begin

No single path is more "correct" than another. The best starting point is the intersection of your existing skills and genuine curiosity. Prompting and workflow integration have the lowest barrier; model creation and safety research demand years of specialization.

Paths Overlap

The most effective AI practitioners combine 2–3 paths. An MLOps engineer who understands agentic systems. A prompt engineer who can read a research paper. A data engineer who thinks about alignment. Breadth compounds with depth.

The Field Moves

What's cutting-edge today becomes standard infrastructure in 18 months. The meta-skill across all paths is learning velocity — the ability to absorb new frameworks, concepts, and capabilities faster than the field evolves.
